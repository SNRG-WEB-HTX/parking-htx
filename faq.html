For Property Owners
Does PARKING HTX charge property owners a monthly fee?

No. PARKING HTX operates on a revenue-share model. We invest in your property — signage, technology, and full operations — and earn a percentage of parking revenue generated. Property owners pay zero upfront and zero monthly. If your lot isn't generating revenue, neither are we. It's that simple.

What size properties does PARKING HTX manage?

We manage parking operations for commercial properties of all sizes — from a 20-space surface lot to multi-level structured garages with 2,000+ spaces. We also serve event venues, medical campuses, restaurant districts, mixed-use developments, and hospitality properties across Greater Houston.

How long does setup take after signing?

After a management agreement is signed, most properties are fully operational within 7–14 days. We handle signage installation, HONK payment zone configuration, full payment infrastructure setup, and driver instruction materials. You don't need to coordinate a single thing — we manage it all.

How do I receive my monthly revenue?

Revenue is disbursed monthly via ACH direct deposit, accompanied by a transparent, itemized report showing total transactions, gross revenue, operating costs, and your net payout. You always have full visibility into what your property is earning and exactly how it was calculated — no guesswork, no surprises.

What kind of reporting will I receive?

PARKING HTX provides clear monthly reports covering transaction totals, activity trends, peak demand periods, revenue by zone or area, and performance comparisons. Every dollar is easy to understand. We believe full transparency is the foundation of a good owner relationship.

Can PARKING HTX integrate with our current system?

Yes. We review your current setup, existing signage, access flow, validation needs, and reporting processes to build a clean transition plan. Most transitions are seamless and don't require you to scrap existing infrastructure — we work around and improve what's already there.

What areas of Houston does PARKING HTX serve?

We serve all of Greater Houston including Downtown, Midtown, Montrose, The Heights, EaDo, Medical Center, Galleria, River Oaks, Greenway Plaza, the Energy Corridor, Katy, Sugar Land, Pearland, and surrounding communities. Houston is our home market and we know every corridor.

What makes PARKING HTX different from national operators?

National operators move slowly, charge property owners regardless of performance, and treat Houston like every other city in their portfolio. PARKING HTX is local-owned, revenue-share only, and built around Houston's specific neighborhoods, events, and demand patterns. You work directly with decision-makers — not a regional account manager based two cities away.

For Drivers
Do I need to download an app to pay for parking?

No app required. Simply scan the QR code on our signage using your phone's camera, or text the posted zone number to initiate payment. The entire process completes in under 30 seconds from any smartphone. This frictionless experience means you're parked and moving faster — no downloads, no accounts, no waiting.

What payment methods are accepted?

PARKING HTX lots powered by HONK Mobility accept all major credit cards and debit cards through the mobile payment flow. Payment is processed securely through HONK's platform — no cash handling required at the lot level. Your receipt is delivered digitally immediately after payment.

How does the QR code payment process work?

Look for the PARKING HTX sign posted in the lot. Scan the QR code with your phone camera (no app needed), follow the on-screen prompts, enter your payment details, and you're done. You'll receive a confirmation and can extend your session if needed — all from your phone.

What if I have trouble paying or need help?

The PARKING HTX signage at each location displays a phone number and zone number for assistance. You can also reach us directly at 346-291-6010 or email info@parking-htx.com. Our team is responsive and committed to resolving any driver issues quickly.

¿Parking HTX cobra una tarifa mensual a los propietarios?

No. Parking HTX opera con un modelo de participación en ingresos. Invertimos en señalización, tecnología y operaciones, y ganamos un porcentaje de los ingresos generados. Sin costo inicial y sin tarifa mensual.

¿Qué tamaño de propiedades maneja Parking HTX?

Manejamos lotes comerciales pequeños, garajes estructurados, centros médicos, venues de eventos, desarrollos mixtos, restaurantes y propiedades de hospitalidad en el Gran Houston.

¿Cuánto tarda la instalación?

Después de firmar el acuerdo, la mayoría de propiedades pueden estar operando en 7 a 14 días. Nos encargamos de señalización, configuración HONK e infraestructura de pago.

¿Cómo recibo mis ingresos?

Los ingresos se pagan mensualmente por depósito directo ACH con un reporte claro de transacciones, ingresos brutos, costos operativos y pago neto.

¿Qué hace diferente a Parking HTX?

Somos una empresa local de Houston, enfocada en resultados, respuesta rápida y operaciones diseñadas alrededor del mercado real de Houston.
