Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers for property owners.

PARKING HTX is built around simple terms, transparent numbers, and a true partnership model that helps owners turn parking into a better-performing asset.

What does PARKING HTX do?
PARKING HTX helps property owners monetize, organize, regulate, and improve their parking assets. We provide the parking plan, signage, mobile payment setup, customer support, enforcement coordination, performance tracking, and ongoing optimization so the property can produce revenue without the owner having to operate a parking company.
Is there really no upfront cost to the property owner?
Yes. The partnership is structured so PARKING HTX handles the setup at no upfront cost to the owner. That can include signage strategy, payment setup, operating structure, customer-facing instructions, support workflows, and enforcement coordination. The goal is simple: reduce owner risk and create a performance-based partnership where the property earns when the parking performs.
How does the revenue share work?
PARKING HTX uses an owner-first revenue share structure. The property owner receives the lion's share of the revenue, and PARKING HTX succeeds by helping the property perform better. Exact terms depend on the property, location, demand, enforcement needs, technology setup, and operating complexity.
What types of properties can PARKING HTX evaluate?
We can evaluate small surface lots, retail centers, restaurants, entertainment-area lots, mixed-use properties, private lots, garages, underused parking areas, and larger commercial assets. The model can work for anything from a 5-space surface lot to a 2,000-car garage when the location, demand, and parking behavior make sense.
Will this hurt my customers or tenants?
The goal is the opposite. A clean parking program can reduce confusion, improve turnover, protect spaces for real customers, and create a more professional arrival experience. PARKING HTX focuses on clear signs, simple scan-to-pay instructions, customer support, and smart enforcement so the system feels organized instead of aggressive.
Do owners get access to real-time numbers?
Yes. Transparency is central to the model. Owners can receive visibility into revenue, activity, transactions, occupancy patterns, and performance reporting so they are not guessing about how the parking asset is doing.
Who handles signs, technology, support, and enforcement?
PARKING HTX manages the parking operation details. That includes signage planning, payment-flow setup, parker instructions, support processes, enforcement coordination, reporting, and ongoing adjustments. The owner does not need to build a parking department or manage the daily friction.
Who is behind PARKING HTX?
PARKING HTX was founded by Bobby Frilot III, CPP, a Houston-based parking professional with more than 15 years of experience across multiple parking markets throughout the United States.

Bobby has worked directly with property owners, operators, developers, and parking technology platforms to improve operations, strengthen revenue performance, organize enforcement, and create a better experience for the people actually using the parking. That experience shaped the PARKING HTX approach: transparent partnerships, clean implementation, real-time visibility, and parking programs that are built to perform without creating extra work for the property owner.

PARKING HTX was built around a simple standard: parking should be professionally managed, easy for customers to use, and valuable enough for owners to see it as an asset — not a side problem that only gets attention when something goes wrong.
How fast can a property get started?
Most locations can be fully onboarded and live within 1–2 weeks from approval. That timeline includes property evaluation, revenue strategy, signage planning, payment setup, testing, enforcement configuration, customer-flow review, and final launch.

Some smaller surface lots can move faster, while larger garages or more complex properties may require additional coordination. The priority is speed with precision: a clean launch, working technology, clear signage, and a parking experience that feels organized from day one.
How do I find out if my lot is a fit?
Call 346-291-6010 or email info@parking-htx.com. PARKING HTX can review the property, discuss demand, and explain whether a no-cost parking partnership makes sense.
